Interesting Keyword Searches

Posted by: Tue May 27, 2008

I was browsing through my Google Analytics stats earlier and thought it’d be interesting to browse through search terms that resulted in a hit on my site.

Man, there are some crazy entries!

Here’s a list of some of my favourites:

  • “break the build” punishment - Good to see people taking the hard line on build breakers.
  • “good code” - Yeah like you’ll find any of that here.
  • “when they play in their garden” - WTF?
  • camera keeps dropping out of Skype - Just keeping putting it back in.
  • code to add a spade to your writing - I would love to see that code.
  • fartfox - This hit makes sense because I have actually written about this, but why would someone search for it?!
  • getroman - That’s my superhero name.
  • optus bastards - You got that right!
  • yoooooootube
I’m amazed that some of those searches ended up here.

The Internet is one crazy place ;)
