Random Resolutions

Published 2008-12-27 by OJ Reeves

Read time: 1 min

I’ve never been one to make New Years Resolutions. I think they’re a bit of a wank to be honest. But I do love a good gimmick. So when I stumbled on this I both loved and hated it.

This first attempt made me chuckle. I think all the time, let alone at inappropriate times. The bigger question is: what should I be thinking about at inappropriate times?.

Who knows.

After this second one appeared I thought that this thing knew more about me than I felt comfortable about. So I stopped right there.

This is quite a neat little Silverlight app. One that I think most people will get a kick out of, even if it’s just for a few minutes. Enjoy.
